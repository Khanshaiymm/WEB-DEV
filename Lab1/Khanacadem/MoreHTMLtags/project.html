Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 1 tbsp
Oil 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, milk, egg, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan and add a little oil.
  3. Pour batter into the pan.
  4. Cook both sides until golden.

Simple veggie salad

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Cucumber 1
Tomatoes 2
Onion 1/2
Olive oil 1 tbsp
Salt To taste

Steps:

  1. Wash the vegetables.
  2. Cut cucumber, tomatoes, and onion into small pieces.
  3. Put everything in a bowl.
  4. Add olive oil and salt, then mix.
